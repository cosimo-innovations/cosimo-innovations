Our Motivation

India's EV revolution is creating a
battery waste crisis

The same batteries powering India's clean transport future are becoming tomorrow's toxic waste — unless we act now. That's why Cosimo Innovations was born.

⚡
India's EV Boom
India sold over 1.7 million EVs in FY2024 alone. Every EV battery will eventually need end-of-life management — creating millions of tonnes of Li-ion waste by 2030.
🌍
Import Dependency
India imports nearly 100% of its lithium. Recovering lithium from used batteries domestically could dramatically reduce our dependence on geopolitically sensitive supply chains.
☠️
Hazardous Disposal
Unregulated battery disposal leaches toxic heavy metals — cobalt, lead, nickel — into soil and groundwater. The Environmental & human health cost is enormous.
📋
Regulatory Push
Battery Waste Management Rules 2022 mandate EPR targets for producers. We help companies meet compliance, earn carbon credits, and turn obligation into opportunity.

Our Recycling Process

01
Reverse Logistics Collection
Doorstep battery collection from EV fleets, OEMs, and consumers across India using our tech-enabled logistics network.
02
AI-Assisted SoH Grading
Each battery is graded for State-of-Health. High-SoH packs are routed to second-life applications; low-SoH packs go to recycling.
03
Low-Carbon Hydrometallurgy
Our proprietary wet-chemical process recovers 95%+ lithium, cobalt, nickel, and manganese at battery-grade purity — without toxic emissions.
04
Digital Marketplace Listing
Recovered materials and second-life packs are listed on LoopCell, our B2B marketplace, for direct purchase by battery manufacturers and ESS developers.

How Carbon Credits Flow

1
Baseline Measurement
We establish the conventional processing baseline (pyrometallurgy / virgin mining) for each material stream to calculate the emissions delta.
2
Process Monitoring & MRV
Real-time monitoring of energy use, chemical inputs, and material outputs feeds into our Measurement, Reporting & Verification (MRV) system.
3
Third-Party Verification
Credits are verified under Gold Standard, Verra VCS, or India's ICM–BEE framework by accredited third-party auditors.
4
Marketplace Trading via LoopCell
Verified credits are listed on LoopCell's Carbon Registry for purchase by corporates seeking to offset Scope 3 emissions in their supply chains.

Our Vision

Where we're building
towards

Cosimo Innovations envisions a future where no Li-ion battery in India ends up in a landfill — and every EV powers a genuinely clean energy cycle.

01
India's Circular Battery Hub
Build the most comprehensive battery circular economy infrastructure in India — from collection logistics to processing to materials trading — reducing the country's critical mineral import bill by ₹10,000+ crore annually.
02
Net-Zero Battery Supply Chain
Make the Indian battery supply chain carbon-negative by 2035 — where the combination of recycled materials, avoided emissions, and tradeable carbon credits results in a net carbon sink rather than a source.
03
LoopCell: Asia's Battery Exchange
Grow LoopCell into the leading digital marketplace for secondary battery materials across South and Southeast Asia — creating transparent price discovery, standardized grading, and liquid markets for recovered lithium and carbon credits.
04
Democratized EPR Compliance
Make regulatory compliance effortless for every EV brand, battery maker, and logistics company in India — through plug-and-play collection programs, automated CPCB reporting, and shared processing infrastructure.
05
Deep Tech in Battery Diagnostics
Invest in AI-powered battery health prediction, electrochemical fingerprinting, and rapid non-destructive testing — so every battery decision is data-driven, maximising value extraction at every stage of life.
06
Catalysing India's Green Finance
Position battery recycling as a new asset class for ESG-focused capital in India — enabling institutional investors, green bonds, and blended finance to flow into critical material circularity at scale.
