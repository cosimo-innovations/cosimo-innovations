How We Close the Loop

The Scrap-to-Value Marketplace

We make securing end-of-life batteries seamless. Through our dedicated digital marketplace, we connect directly with scrap dealers and strategic partners to safely collect and transport materials.

We Check It Before We Break It

Not every "dead" battery is completely drained. We rigorously test incoming packs to determine if they can be given a second life in alternative energy storage.

State-of-the-Art Processing

Batteries at the end of their usable life are routed to our advanced processing plant, where we safely extract critical, high-purity metals like lithium, cobalt, and nickel.

A True Circular Economy

We keep valuable materials in the supply chain, reducing the industry's reliance on new mining and keeping hazardous materials out of landfills.
